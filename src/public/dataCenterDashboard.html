Metrics Information

PUE (Power Usage Effectiveness): A measure of how efficiently a data center uses energy; ideally close to 1.

CUE (Carbon Usage Effectiveness): Represents the carbon emissions per unit of energy consumed.

WUE (Water Usage Effectiveness): Measures the amount of water used by a data center for cooling per unit of energy consumed.
